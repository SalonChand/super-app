Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Connect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: username, email address, password (hashed)
  • Profile info: profile photo, cover photo, bio, birthday (optional)
  • Content: posts, stories, comments, messages you create
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, device type
  • Device info: push notification tokens for sending notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Connect service
  • To send push notifications you've opted into
  • To show you relevant content and friend suggestions
  • To detect and prevent spam, abuse and security issues

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Cloudinary — for image and video storage
  • Service providers needed to operate the app
  • When required by law

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account, which removes all your data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data — view your profile and posts
  • Delete your data — delete your account in Settings
  • Opt out of notifications — disable in Settings → Preferences

6. Children's Privacy

Connect is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Push notification keys are stored securely.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the app or at our support email.
